About BDTRIP

We are passionate about showcasing the beauty and cultural richness of Bangladesh to the world.

Our Journey Begins

Founded in 2018, BDTRIP emerged from a deep love for our country's rich cultural tapestry and breathtaking natural landscapes. What started as a small initiative to promote local tourism has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting travelers with authentic Bangladeshi experiences.

We believe that every corner of Bangladesh has a story to tell, and we're committed to making these stories accessible to both domestic and international travelers through BDTRIP.

  • 5+ years of excellence in tourism
  • 50,000+ satisfied travelers
  • 150+ unique destinations covered

BDTRIP Mission & Vision

Mission: To make Bangladesh's cultural and natural heritage accessible to everyone through BDTRIP while promoting sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities and preserve our environment for future generations.

Vision: To position Bangladesh as a premier destination for cultural and eco-tourism in South Asia through BDTRIP, known for its authentic experiences, warm hospitality, and commitment to sustainable development.

We work closely with local communities to ensure that tourism becomes a positive force for economic growth while maintaining the integrity of our cultural heritage and natural resources.

BDTRIP Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at BDTRIP

Sustainability

We prioritize eco-friendly practices and support local communities to ensure tourism benefits everyone while preserving our natural environment.

Authenticity

We believe in providing genuine experiences that reflect the true culture and spirit of Bangladesh, not just commercial tourist attractions.

Community Focus

Local communities are at the heart of what we do. We work to ensure they benefit directly from the tourism we promote through BDTRIP.

Excellence

We're committed to providing the highest quality experiences through BDTRIP, from planning to execution, ensuring every journey is memorable.
